TUDO GIRA AO REDOR DE LISTENING (OUVIR)

Yes, listening to English is the most important thing you can do to speak naturally.
Listening weighs MUCH more than ‘studying grammar’, ‘exercising’, and even ‘practicing conversation’ on the road to fluency, and I think if more people really understood that, more people would be expressing themselves better in English.
As you will see in the next lesson, the “thickness” of a language is acquired unconsciously, as you hear things you understand in that language.
A language is something too complex to be automated through conscious processes such as study and memorization.
